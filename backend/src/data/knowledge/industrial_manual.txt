Industrial IoT Maintenance Manual

Safety Protocols:
1. Always disconnect power before maintenance
2. Use proper PPE (gloves, safety glasses)
3. Follow lockout/tagout procedures

Common Issues and Solutions:
- Sensor Failure: Check wiring connections first, then replace sensor
- Connectivity Issues: Verify network configuration and signal strength
- Data Drift: Recalibrate sensors every 6 months

Preventive Maintenance Schedule:
- Daily: Visual inspection of sensors
- Weekly: Clean sensor surfaces
- Monthly: Check data accuracy
- Quarterly: Full system calibration
